Overview

The VA Prison System Training Portal provides a seamless learning experience for veterans and prisoners.

Purpose

The portal aids in rehabilitation through education, skill-building, and mentorship.

Overall Architecture and UI Design

The training portal is a web-based platform accessible on computers, tablets, and secure devices.

Responsive design ensures accessibility across various devices with a simple and intuitive user interface.

The home screen provides access to sections such as Courses, Certifications, Mentorship, Community, Progress Tracking, and Support.

User Roles and Dashboards

Prisoners/Veterans

Personalized dashboard for progress tracking, enrolled courses, and achievements.

Non-Profit Organizations

Program management dashboard and impact reporting tools.

VA and Corrections Authorities

Admin dashboard with engagement metrics, course enrollment, and recidivism data.

Employers and Re-entry Programs

Access to a talent database and job posting features.

Key Features - Learning Opportunities

  • Learning Management System (LMS)
  • Multilingual Support
  • Offline Access
  • Certification Pathways

Key Features - Data & Analytics

Real-time analytics for engagement rates, course completions, and recidivism tracking.

Key Features - Support & Coaching

Virtual Mentorship Rooms, Community Forum, and integration with non-profits for collaborative opportunities.

Conclusion and Next Steps

This presentation outlined the key design elements and features of the VA Prison System Training Portal. Next steps include prototyping, testing, and launching the portal.

Call to Action: Collaboration with the VA, corrections, and non-profit partners is essential for successful deployment.
